Reheat Tips

Oven
(Crispy texture)

Preheat the oven to 180°C. Lightly spray the bread with water, then heat for about 5–8 minutes until the crust becomes crisp again.

Steam
(Chewy texture)

Bring water to a boil, then lower to medium heat. Place the bread in a steamer for 3–5 minutes until soft and warm with a chewy texture.

Avocado and Egg
Sourdough Sandwich

  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • Red pepper flakes
  1. 1. Heat olive oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  2. 2. Crack eggs into the pan, season with salt and pepper.
  3. 3. Cook eggs for 2-3 minutes until whites are set but yolks are still runny.
  4. 4. While eggs cook, toast sourdough slices until golden.
  5. 5. Mash avocado onto one toast slice, season lightly with salt.
  6. 6. Place cooked eggs on top of the avocado.
  7. 7. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes for heat.
  8. 8. Top with the second toast slice.
  9. 9. Cut in half and serve immediately.

Classic Grilled Cheese Sourdough

  • 2 thick slices of sourdough bread
  • 56g sharp cheddar, freshly grated
  • 56g mozzarella, freshly grated
  • 2 tablespoons softened butter
  • Salt and pepper
  1. 1. Warm the skillet over medium-low heat.
  2. 2. While heating, mix freshly grated cheeses.
  3. 3. Butter one side of each bread slice completely.
  4. 4. Place the first slice butter-side down in a warm skillet.
  5. 5. Add cheese mixture and sprinkle with salt.
  6. 6. Top with the second slice, butter-side up.
  7. 7. Cook for 4-5 minutes until golden brown underneath.
  8. 8. Carefully flip using two spatulas.
  9. 9. Cook another 3-4 minutes until golden and cheese is melted.

Peanut Butter & Banana
Sourdough Toastie

  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 2 tbsps Peanut Butter
  • 1 ripe banana, sliced
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Butter
  • Cinammon
  1. 1. Spread peanut butter on both slices.
  2. 2. Sliced banana 3-4mm thick enough slices to cover bread but still have some peanut butter showing through.
  3. 3. A pinch of cinnamon - sprinkled.
  4. 4. Drizzle with honey.
  5. 5. Cook both sides until golden.

Chicken Pesto
Sourdough Sandwich

  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 1 cooked chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of pesto sauce
  • 2 slices of mozzarella cheese
  • 1 handful of fresh spinach leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  1. 1. Put a frying pan on a medium heat, brush one side of each slice of sourdough with olive oil.
  2. 2. Spread pesto sauce evenly on the un-brushed side of both slices of bread.
  3. 3. Put slices of chicken breast, mozzarella cheese and fresh spinach leaves on one slice of bread.
  4. 4. Add salt and pepper to taste, then top with other slice of bread, pesto side down.
  5. 5. Put the sandwich in the grill pan and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side or until the bread is lightly browned and the cheese is melted.
  6. 6. Remove from pan, slice in half if desired, and serve immediately.
