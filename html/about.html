ABOUT US

OUR STORY

History of Bupang Bakery

Bupang Bakery was founded by Ms. Foo in Kulai in 2021 as a one-woman journey in a small home kitchen. With nothing but passion and patience, she hand-shaped every loaf and bagel using traditional slow-fermentation methods.

In 2024, she opened her own baking studio, continuing her commitment to handmade, low-sugar, oil-free breads — crafted with care, from start to finish.


Mission

To raise awareness of natural yeast in Malaysia by offering handmade, healthy baked goods that satisfy cravings without compromising well-being.

Vision

To make natural yeast more accessible and better understood by Malaysians by showcasing its benefits through high-quality products, education, and meaningful branding.
